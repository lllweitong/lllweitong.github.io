Bio

I'm a finial year PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, advised by Tijay Chung. Before joining VT, I received my bechelor's degree from Zhejiang University.

I am broadly interested in measurement, security, and networking. My research focuses on measuring and securing Internet protocols including routing (like BGP and RPKI), Email, and DNS.
